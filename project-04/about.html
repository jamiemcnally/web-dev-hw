About Bundle of Daisies

Hi there, and welcome to Bundle of Daisies! My name is Jamie McNally, and I'm a beekeeper based out of the beautiful Ovando, Montana valley. I've had a passion and respect for honey bees for as long as I can remember, and I'm so fortunate to see them at work everyday. I started my first hive in the summer of 2022, and I can't wait to add more to the mix. You can catch me out collecting honey in my pink beesuit!
